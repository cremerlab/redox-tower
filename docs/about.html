About

ElectronFlow is an interactive companion to the redox tower framework described in the paper below. It lets researchers and students explore how thermodynamic feasibility of microbial metabolisms shifts with environmental conditions — pH, gas partial pressures, and metabolite concentrations — by applying the Nernst equation to each half-reaction in real time.

Paper

A. Scarampi, J. Cremer, and O. S. Soyer et al. Community Metabolism in Dynamic Redox Landscapes. Under revision.

This interactive website is also available on GitHub (cremerlab/redox-tower). The app runs entirely in the browser via Pyodide and Panel — no server required.
